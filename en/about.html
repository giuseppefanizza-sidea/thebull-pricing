About us

Over 25 years producing shaped rebar and precast concrete. 4 facilities, 2 countries, one quality standard.

Our story

From local producer to international group

Ferramati was founded in Fasano (BR) as a rebar shaping centre serving construction companies in Puglia. Over more than 25 years, the company expanded its offering to become an international group with 4 production facilities across Italy and Romania.

Today Ferramati International supplies a complete range of structural products — from shaped rebar to precast concrete, to EPS systems — with a single technical and commercial contact for the entire construction site.

Growth has been driven by one clear principle: offering construction companies a partner that simplifies the structural supply chain without compromising on quality.

Our facilities

Fasano (BR) — Italy

Historic headquarters and main shaping centre. Production of shaped and assembled rebar, FERCASS, and logistics coordination for Southern Italy.

Verdello (BG) — Italy

Northern Italy facility. Shaped rebar, lattice slabs and double slabs. Direct service to Lombardy, Veneto and Emilia-Romagna.

Timișoara — Romania

Production unit for the western Romanian market. Shaped rebar and precast concrete to the same Italian quality standards.

Oradea — Romania

Second Romanian facility. EPS element production and STEP systems for civil and industrial construction in Romania and neighbouring markets.
